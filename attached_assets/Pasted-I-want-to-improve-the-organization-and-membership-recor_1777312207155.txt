I want to improve the organization and membership records for readability and debugging.

Important:
Do not break Firestore security rules. Keep the membership document ID as:
<userId>_<organizationId>

Example:
dERLgNAke8O15YPanLtDNZ9sC6l1_org_yorku

But inside the membership document, always store a readableId field:
mbr_<schoolSlug>_<role>_<emailSlug>

Example:
mbr_yorku_admin_ariyan_azami84_gmail_com

Also, I want the organizations collection to contain clear summary information.
